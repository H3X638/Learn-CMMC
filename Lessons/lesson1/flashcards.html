Click each card to reveal the answer.

What is the focus of CMMC Level 1?
Protects Federal Contract Information (FCI) with 17 basic FAR practices. Self-assessed annually.
Which level aligns with NIST SP 800-171?
Level 2 – Protects CUI with 110 controls. May require third-party assessment.
What distinguishes Level 3 from lower levels?
Government-verified, based on NIST SP 800-172, for advanced persistent threat protection.
Key difference between Levels 1, 2, and 3?
Level 1: FCI / Level 2: CUI / Level 3: High-value CUI.
Why are CMMC Levels important?
They align security maturity with data sensitivity, protecting the defense supply chain.
